About

I am a seasoned software engineer with strong experience in integration solutions. I primarily use Java and have extensive experience in implementing system integration solutions (backend) Java 8 (Spring Boot) and Javascript (NodeJS). I also managed the ability to handle various activities, priorities and problem-solving.

As an SME Integration, I currently qualified as a generalist solutions architect and capable to develop any kind of integration solutions (ETL, EAI, SOA, API/Webservice).

I obtained extensive experience in coding, requirements’ analysis and specification, designing, data modeling, testing, among others which enabled me to acquire a very good understanding of all steps of the software development cycle.

The most experience is in the Telecom industry, including experience in Service Oriented Architecture, mostly on integration projects, performing as a Java developer and integration solution architect.

I had the opportunity to work with offshore development teams and consultants from other cultures and I proved high ability to participate in multi-lingual meetings as well as to integrate with an international environment with a rapid self-starting capability and experience in team working.

While working in the largest Brazilian company in the oil industry, I provided guidance on technical processes and oversaw IT activities to track progress and ensure that projects tasks are completed efficiently and correctly, identifying risks and complexities as well as developing mitigation strategies. As a solution architect, I designed the scope of integration work (SOA) and support for Enterprise Service Bus, included conceptual solution options, diagrams, and workflow to support a compelling solution.

Fast learner and a highly adjustable professional, I am always kept up-to-date with new technologies and the best practices in software engineering.
